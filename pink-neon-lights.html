Pink Neon Lights

-Caleb Hess


1Everytime I think of you and when we were happy together,
2I see myself walking down a short dim corridor, then
3into the room I find myself longing to be in again the most -
4in it with you and I in bed, and kissing under the pink neon lights.


5I've taken for granted the warm smiles and the even warmer embraces
6that were so plentiful then, now even the thought of them
7sends chills to my nape and shakes me to my core.


8Those were the years squeezed into months and the days stretched to forever.
9Those were sweet memories now with a bittersweet flavor.
10They warm my heart for a moment but a cold flood of regret and realization
11drowns out the warm and chills my blood like cold arctic waters.
12The fear of frostbite compels me to remind myself always of the love I have for you
13and our memories in that heavenly neon pink palace


14Oh how I wish for the days we bathed in that pink neon haze.
15I know that I was just too amazed at your love and how you showed it in so many ways
16like when I woke up to you and a hand covered in glaze
16from the donuts you ordered from that place we loved that wasn't too far away.


17I would be wrong if I said I'm not the reason we lost so many days of happiness and love in that neon pink place.
18I would be lying if I said I didn't care that you're not next to me now
19with that same look in your eyes from my memory of us under pink neon lights.
